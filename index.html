How to Become Eligible for the Nuur Airdrop

To be eligible for the Nuur Airdrop, you must meet the following criteria:

  • Step 1: You must have bought Nuur tokens during the presale market.
  • Step 2: You must have held at least 500,000 Nuur tokens in your wallet for more than a month.
  • Step 3: You must use the same wallet address that was used to purchase the tokens during the presale.

If you meet all the above criteria, you can check your eligibility and claim your airdrop tokens!
